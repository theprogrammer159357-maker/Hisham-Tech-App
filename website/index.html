About El Haramin Hoops

El Haramin Hoops is an ambitious basketball game developed with extreme effort and patience. Created by Mohamed Hesham Mohamed Anwar Qutb, this game celebrates the talented players of El Haramin Youth Center.

Built with cutting-edge technology including Gemini AI integration, the game delivers an immersive AAA-quality experience that brings basketball to life on your mobile device.
